HTML5 Canvas Game

W, S, A, D - Movement

Mouse - Aim and shoot

1, 2, 3, 4 - increase stat

L - turn off music

Survive through waves of enemies and gain experience. You level up when you get enough experience and you can increase one of your stats, which you can see in the upper right corner.

There are 3 levels - the first two are with waves of enemies and if you manage to beat them, a boss level awaits. In order to kill the boss, you need to strategically increase your stats while levelling.
